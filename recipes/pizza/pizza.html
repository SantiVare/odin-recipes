Classical Italian Pizza

When we talk about an authentic italian pizza, there can be no other than the original recipe from Napoli.

Although very simple in premise, technique and experience will be required to make a pizza that truly manages to evoke the flavors and subtleties of the italian masterpiece.

Ingredients:

  • 360 grams of filtered water
  • 600 grams of refined flour
  • 1.8 grams of bread yeast
  • 12 grams of salt
  • Necessary amount of mozzarella cheese
  • Necessary amount of tomato sauce
  • Necessary amount of basil

Steps

  1. Mix the yeast with the water, and stir with a spoon until dissolved.
  2. Unify the flour with the salt and pour the water onto the flour.
  3. Mix until you form a solid dough
  4. Kneed the dough until you feel the gluten has developed. (The dough should be quite elastic and shouldn't be separated easily)
  5. Let the dough rest in an hermetic container for three to four hours.
  6. When properly fermented, divide into two separate balls of dough and let it rest in the fridge for 12/24 hours.
  7. Before you decide to cook the pizza, take the dough out of the fridge for three hours or until room temperature
  8. Put the oven at maximum (450Â°C max) and preheat for 25 minutes.
  9. Take a ball of dough and give it the form of a pizza, leaving some air at the borders
  10. Put it in the oven and cook until done. (Depends on the temperature of your oven)
  11. Enjoy.
